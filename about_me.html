About Me

Hi there, my name is Prashant. I love to lead, read, write, take photos, travel, teach, spend quality time with my friends & family, and play basketball.

If I had to distill my passion for life down into a couple of key principles, I think they would be:

1. Witnessing, capturing, and expressing the beauty present in life.
2. Sharing that beauty with others.

I enjoy working on projects and initiatives that have a clear, meaningful impact on others and love observing where visionary thinking meets brilliant execution.

I'm currently an Associate Product Manager (APM) at Google, where I work on improving Gaming experiences in YouTube App Ads.
